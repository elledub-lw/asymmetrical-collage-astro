Military radio operators practice signal discipline. They transmit only what's necessary, when necessary, to whom necessary. Everything else is noise that compromises the mission.

The average person broadcasts constantly. Social media, messages, opinions, transmitted without asking whether the receiver needs it or whether it matters.

Signal discipline isn't about saying less. It's about transmitting only what moves things forward.
