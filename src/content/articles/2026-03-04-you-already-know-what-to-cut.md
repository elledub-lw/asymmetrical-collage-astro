You already know what to cut. The knowledge was never the bottleneck.

The commitments that drain you, the subscriptions you never use, the relationships that only take, you identified these months ago. You just haven't acted.

Permission is the bottleneck. Not information. Not frameworks.
Stop researching what to cut. Start cutting what you know should go.
