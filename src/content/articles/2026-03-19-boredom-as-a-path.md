The stream never stops.

One more headline. One more notification. One more thing that needed your attention ten seconds ago.

You stay on top of it. You call it being informed.

Boredom has gone missing. So has the thinking that used to live there.

I walk without headphones. The silence is loud at first. Then it settles and something shifts. An idea surfaces. A decision clarifies. A question you didn't know you were asking finds its answer.

You can't schedule that. You can only make room for it.

Clear the space. The ideas know where to find you.
