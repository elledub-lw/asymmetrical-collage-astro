About

Asymmetrical Collage is a daily blog and holding company run by Laurent Waisman.

I write about sovereignty, resilience, Bitcoin, risk, and building for the long term. One post a day. Each one tries to say one thing clearly.

I have spent twenty years in banking, fintech, and Bitcoin — risk management, compliance, information security, payments, custody. I co-founded nodl, a Bitcoin hardware and infrastructure company.

I am not trying to tell you what to think. I am trying to offer a framework for thinking with — the kind I wish someone had handed me earlier.
